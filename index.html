Researcher in Multi-modal Learning

This is Razieh /ˈrɑːziːjə/. I'm a PhD researcher supervised by Prof. Hilde Kuehne at the University of Tuebingen, Germany. My research is mainly on Multi-modal Large Language Models with a focus on reseasoning.


I had the honor of collaborating with postdocs at Prof. Philip Torr's Vision Group at the University of Oxford, and also working at Prof. Zeynep Akata's EML lab at the Technical University of Munich as an AI researcher. If you'd like to discuss ideas, feel free to reach out to me either on LinkedIn or via email: razieh92rezaei@gmail.com.
